DESIGNER, MAKER, HACKER, ENTREPRENEUR.

  •

  •  
  • Email Me

After 12 years on design industry, working mainly as art director and creative director I started to look for something beyond the looks. Defining shapes and colours, making that girl look even hotter or setting the perfect light and reflex were not my only priorities anymore. Clients demanded results, innovation and high-tech! I took a hand-full of code skills, some nerdy robotics knowledge and mixed with the design thinking I was so in to. What easily started as art now has become the fuel of my life. The challenge is to make it simple, measurable and most important profitable. I don't believe in impossible anymore. With the right incentive, there are no limits to create something amazing. If you agree, or got stuck somehow give me a call. We can work it out!
